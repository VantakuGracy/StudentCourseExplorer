India has a total of 4,658 engineering colleges offering Bachelor of Technology (BTech) courses in various disciplines. Out of these colleges, there are 4,151 top BTech colleges in India including the government and public colleges. The number of students applying for the course has increased significantly in recent years as the scope of BTech is extremely wide, which has led to the division of the course into many specializations.

The engineering domain offers a vast array of opportunities for those interested in creating, innovating, and problem-solving. Joining the engineering field opens doors to a dynamic career where you can contribute to technological advancements, design solutions for complex challenges, and make a tangible impact on society. Engineering is a highly sought-after career in India, with millions of options available to students. The stream offers various courses such as B.E/B.Tech, Diploma courses, and integrated engineering, catering to the diverse needs of the industry.

BE/BTech is a 4-year undergraduate course in various specializations like Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, etc. A diploma in Engineering is a 3-year course students pursue after class 10/12. MTech is a 2-year postgraduate course offered to candidates with an undergraduate degree in engineering.
