BSc stands for Bachelor of Science is a 3-year undergraduate course offered in the Science stream. Candidates who have successfully passed their 10+2 are eligible to apply for BSc Course. Science Courses are a combination of various subjects opted by candidates after 10th or 12th grade out of the subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology. Science Courses have always been the most sought-after courses in India.

The most popular Science Courses in India are degree courses such as Bachelor of Science, Master of Science, Diploma in Science, Doctorate (Ph.D.) in Science, etc.
